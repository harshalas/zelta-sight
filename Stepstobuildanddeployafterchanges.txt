Step 1: Save Your Code Changes
Step 2: Clear Out Old Native Artifacts (Crucial if modifying native logic)
	PowerShell
	Remove-Item -Recurse -Force android
Step 3: Run the Prebuild Checklist
	Bash
	npx expo prebuild --platform android --clean
Step 4: Fire Off the Expo Cloud Build (EAS)
	Bash
	eas build --platform android --profile development --clear-cache
Step 5: Download and Load the App onto Your Device
	Once the terminal shows that the build has completed successfully:

	Grab the APK Link: Look at your terminal output. Expo will provide a QR code and a direct web link (e.g., 	https://expo.dev/artifacts/...).

	Download the File: Click the link on your computer to download the .apk file, or scan the QR code with your phone to download it 	directly onto your device.

	Uninstall Previous Version: If you already have an older version of your app running on your phone, uninstall it first to prevent an 	Android "App not installed" package conflict error.

	Install and Run: Tap the newly downloaded .apk file on your phone. If Google Play Protect asks for permission, click "Install 	Anyway".

	Open the app, enter your Gemini key into your portal lock screen, and your fresh code updates will be live and running instantly!
